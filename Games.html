Games Unit [+..••]

In the games unit we made a 2D topdown plane shooter game from scratch in GameMaker.

The brown planes just shoot regular bullets forward that takes 5 damage if it hits the player, the White planes shoots a targeting bullet/missile that targets/changes direction to face the player every few seconds but can be avoided by flying the missiles into the other planes.
If you fly into another plane that plane will explode and you will lose 10 health
Once a plane goes off screen the game decides a random location along the top of the screen to then place the plane back in an infinite loop until the place is destroyed.

The player has three lives, everytime their health hits Zero they lose a life. If their lives and health hits Zero then the scoreboard pops up and asks for a name input,
it will then save your score and name to the scoreboard and then the game will restart.
